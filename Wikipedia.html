IBM (International Business Machines)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

IBM is one of the oldest and most well-known technology companies in the world. It stands for International Business Machines and is mainly known for creating computer hardware, software, and providing cloud and AI services. The company has its headquarters in the United States.

Background

IBM was founded over 100 years ago, in 1911. It started with making simple business machines and later became one of the biggest names in computing. The name "IBM" was adopted in 1924. Since then, it has played an important role in the development of technology around the world.
